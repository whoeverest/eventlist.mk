Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

When you register on our site, you are given the option to give our website the right to access your Facebook events. Specifically, all the events you are invited to, have created, or you have RSVP'd as "maybe going" or "not going".

We do NOT store events marked as "private" in our database.

Registering is completely optional and you can use all of the site features without giving us access to your events.

What do we use your information for?

The events we gather are aggregated and a filtered list of unique events happening soon is publicly displayed.

User data is not associated with event data when it is shown on our website, meaning the events that are displayed contain no information about the user who provided them.

How do we protect your information?

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All data is stored in a database that is not publically accessible, on a server that has been properly secured.
